Pay Check and Track Services – Easy Payment Tracking & Reporting Tool
Pay Check and Track Services is a powerful yet simple payment tracking application designed to help businesses and professionals manage their online service payments, subscriptions, and billing records with ease. Whether you run a small business, freelance services, or subscription-based platform, this tool helps you stay organized and in control of your finances.





Features
Key Benefits:

📊 Effortless Payment Monitoring – Track all your payments in one place and view them by date, status, or service type.
📅 Smart Reporting – Automatically generate clear weekly PDF reports (from Thursday to Thursday) for accounting or review.
📈 CSV Import & Excel Export – Import payment data from CSV files and export detailed data into Excel for further analysis.
🗂 Reliable Data Management – Uses an XML-based data storage system, making it lightweight and easy to maintain.
📆 Intelligent Scheduling – Takes into account banking working days and weekends, adjusting payment dates when needed.
💼 Perfect for Finance Teams – Helps prepare financial summaries and expense reports quickly, saving time and reducing errors.
How It Works:
This application uses an XML file as its database and allows you to:

Track recurring and one-time payments

Generate reports in PDF (requires a PDF viewer)

Upload reports or raw data to Excel for extended workflows

Import data from CSV for quick setup or bulk updates


Make payment management easier, transparent, and stress-free — Pay, monitor, and stay organized with confidence


Requirements
Adobe Acrobat Reader

Microsoft Excel
